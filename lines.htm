MOVIE LINES

💡「嘿，聽著，沒有人能取代我們。」
Hey, listen, no ones getting replaced.

💡「對這世界來說，你可能只是某人，但對某人來說，你可能是他的全世界。」
To the world you may be just one person, but to one person you may be the whole world.

💡「別因它的結束而哭泣，要為它曾經發生過而微笑。」
Don’t cry because it’s over; smile because it happened.

💡「離開你的人越來越多，所以留下的人才更加重要。」
As more and more people leave you, the more and more important those who remain become.

💡「不是每個道別都可以好好準備；會痛的，往往都曾讓你措手不及。」
Not every goodbye gives you the courtesy to prepare yourself; those that hurt are usually the ones unexpected.

💡「雖然我很堅強，但有時候，還是需要有個人能夠握著我的手跟我說，一切都會越來越好。」
I’m a strong person, but every now and then I also need someone to take my hand and say everything will be okay.

💡「做好你現在的角色。」
Be who you are right now.

💡「如果你總是坐在架子上，那麼你永遠不會被發現。」
If you sit on a shelf for the rest of your life, you’ll never find out.

💡「他與其他牛仔一樣，勇敢、善良、聰明，但胡迪之所以與眾不同，是因為他永遠不會放棄你，
無論發生什麼事，他都會在你身邊。」
He’s brave, like a cowboy should be. And kind and smart. But the thing that makes Woody special, is he’ll never give up on you…ever. He’ll be there for you, no matter what.
